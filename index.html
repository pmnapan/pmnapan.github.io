Ven. Napan Thawornbanjob (Phra Ajarn) is a Buddhist monk dedicated to bridging ancient wisdom and modern sustainability. As Assistant Abbot of Wat Saket (The Golden Mount Temple) in Bangkok, Founding Chair of IBHAP Foundation, and Secretary-General of Religions for Peace Thailand, he advances the 5Ps + Phra framework — mapping Buddhist principles to the UN Sustainable Development Goals across People, Prosperity, Planet, Peace, Partnership, and the unique role of the Sangha (Phra). He is currently completing a PhD in Social Management at Srinakharinwirot University, with a dissertation on "Buddhist Social Marketing to Achieve SDGs."
